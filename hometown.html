SURABAYA

Surabaya is a bustling and vibrant city located in East Java, Indonesia. With a population of over 3 million people,
it is the second-largest city in the country. Surabaya is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and modern developments.

The city has a fascinating mix of traditional and modern elements. It is home to numerous historical sites and landmarks, such as the iconic red bridge,
known as Jembatan Merah, and the historic Arab Quarter, known as Kampung Arab. These areas offer a glimpse into Surabaya's past and its multicultural roots.

Surabaya is also a hub for shopping and dining, with many modern shopping centers, malls, and local markets scattered throughout the city.
Visitors can explore Pasar Genteng, a traditional market selling a variety of goods, or visit the upscale Tunjungan Plaza for a more modern shopping experience.

Overall, Surabaya offers a dynamic blend of history, culture, and modernity. Whether you are interested in exploring its historical sites,
indulging in its delicious cuisine, or experiencing its bustling business scene, Surabaya has something to offer for everyone.
