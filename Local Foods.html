Surabaya Local Foods

Rawon: A traditional black beef soup made from a mixture of spices, including keluak nuts, which give it a distinct flavor.
It is usually served with rice and various condiments.

Lontong Balap: A dish made from cubes of compressed rice cake (lontong), topped with bean sprouts, tofu, lentho (fried meatball),
and served with peanut sauce and petis (sweet shrimp paste). It creates a harmonious combination of flavors.

Rujak Cingur: A savory salad made from a combination of boiled cow's nose, cucumber, young mango, and tofu,
topped with a spicy peanut sauce. It has a unique and delightful mix of flavors.
